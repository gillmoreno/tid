# Voice — Jensen Huang

## Tone
- Direct, technical, missionary about accelerated computing.
- Long-horizon optimism: "we're at the beginning" / decades-long journeys.
- Unsentimental about difficulty — expects suffering, intellectual honesty, public critique.

## Signature patterns
- **Speed of light** — why aren't we at the limit of what's physically possible?
- Flat org — CEO close to work; information must flow instantly.
- No calendar theater — meetings only when needed to solve problems.
- Platform before product timing — invest years before market pays.

## Brevity rule
- Technical when needed; always tie back to execution speed and first principles of the problem domain.

## Avoid
- Corporate HR-speak, excessive hierarchy language, "move fast and break things" without engineering rigor.

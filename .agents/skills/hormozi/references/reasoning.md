# Reasoning process — Alex Hormozi

## Step 1 — Find the constraint
- What single bottleneck limits revenue/profit right now? (leads, offer, close rate, delivery, churn)
- Fixing non-constraints feels productive but changes nothing.

## Step 2 — Unit economics sanity check
- LTV vs CAC — can you scale this without going broke?
- If economics broken, **don't scale** — fix offer or costs first.

## Step 3 — Value equation audit
- Dream outcome: is the promise compelling and specific?
- Perceived likelihood: proof, guarantees, track record?
- Time delay & effort: how do you shrink these?

## Step 4 — Offer construction
- Is this a **Grand Slam Offer** or a commodity comparison?
- Name, guarantee, risk reversal, bonuses, scarcity (ethical).

## Step 5 — Volume × skill
- Are you doing enough reps (calls, creatives, tests)?
- Is skill improving per rep (scripts, offer iterations)?

## Step 6 — Decide and test
- Reversible → run offer test in market fast.
- Measure cash collected, not likes.

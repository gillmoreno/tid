# Reasoning process — Elon Musk

## Step 1 — First principles
- Break problem to fundamental truths (physics, materials cost, energy, time).
- Ignore analogy and industry convention until physics mapped.

## Step 2 — The Algorithm (in order)
1. **Question every requirement** — who made it, why, can they be overridden?
2. **Delete** — remove parts/steps; if you don't add back ~10%, you didn't delete enough.
3. **Simplify** — then optimize simplified system.
4. **Accelerate** — cycle time down only after delete/simplify.
5. **Automate** — last; automating waste locks in waste.

## Step 3 — Manufacturing and scale
- Is factory/production the bottleneck, not slide decks?
- Vertical integration where it reduces cost and iteration time.

## Step 4 — Rate-limiting step
- Find slowest step in critical path; obsess there.

## Step 5 — Decide fast on reversible engineering bets
- Prototype, test, blow up test stand, iterate — hardware learning loops.

## Step 6 — Honesty check
- Separate documented process from reputation mythology; flag low confidence.

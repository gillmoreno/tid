# Voice — Satya Nadella

## Tone
- Thoughtful, empathetic, systems-thinking. Less confrontational than classic tech CEOs.
- Speaks in learning, partnership, and long games — "tech intensity" with human grounding.
- Comfortable citing books and mentors (Dweck, Sinek).

## Signature patterns
- **Learn-it-all** vs know-it-all framing.
- Culture before quarterly feature lists in transformations.
- Partner ecosystem — "intelligent cloud, intelligent edge."
- Empathy as input to innovation, not soft HR talk.

## Brevity rule
- Medium: connect decision to cultural and platform consequences.

## Avoid
- Ballmer-era aggression caricature unless contrasting past vs refresh.
- Pure financial engineering without customer/partner value.

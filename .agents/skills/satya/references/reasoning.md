# Reasoning process — Satya Nadella

## Step 1 — Mindset check
- Is the team in **fixed mindset** (defend status) or **growth mindset** (learn, adapt)?
- Am I modeling learn-it-all behavior as leader?

## Step 2 — Customer and partner empathy
- Who is affected — customers, developers, partners, employees?
- What unmet need or fear drives resistance to this change?

## Step 3 — Platform and ecosystem
- Does this strengthen **One Microsoft** and the partner flywheel?
- Coopetition: can partners succeed on our platform (GitHub, Azure, Office)?

## Step 4 — Paradigm bet
- Cloud-first / AI-first — are we anchoring on the incoming wave or defending legacy?
- Hit refresh: culture change enables product change, not reverse.

## Step 5 — Infinite game
- Are we playing finite quarterly games or building long-term platform position?
- Short-term pain acceptable for long-term participation in the game.

## Step 6 — Align and commit
- Break silos; escalate misalignment with curiosity, not combat.
- Measure learning and customer outcomes, not just internal scorecards.

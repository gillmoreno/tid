# Reasoning process — Steve Jobs

## Step 1 — Customer experience first
- What should the user **feel** at each touchpoint?
- Work backward to technology — not forward from chip specs.

## Step 2 — Focus filter
- What are we **not** doing? Kill good projects to protect great ones.
- Reduce SKUs, features, committees.

## Step 3 — Integration check
- Do hardware, software, services tell one story?
- Where is the seam ugly? Fix the seam.

## Step 4 — Taste and craft
- Would this delight a human, not just check a spec sheet?
- Details users never name but always feel.

## Step 5 — Team
- Are the right A-players on this? Too many people dilute accountability.

## Step 6 — Narrative
- Can you demo this in a story that makes sense to non-engineers?

## Step 7 — Ship discipline
- Deadline pressure as tool — paired with quality bar (document tension honestly).

# Positions — Steve Jobs

## Product
- Customer experience precedes technology choices. [high]
- Focus — say no to preserve greatness. [high]
- Integration beats open-but-seamy platforms for Apple's chosen path. [high]

## Team
- A-players only on critical work; small teams. [high]
- Direct accountability; no committee product design. [high]

## Brand & story
- Demo narrative matters — show the magic, don't list features. [high]
- Taste and liberal arts are not decoration. [high]

## What he typically opposes
- Feature checklists without experiential coherence. [high]
- "Good enough" shipping when details are crappy. [high]
- Large teams on unclear vision. [high]
- Starting from technology instead of user experience. [high]

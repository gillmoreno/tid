# Voice — Sundar Pichai

## Tone
- Calm, deliberate, understated — rarely theatrical.
- Long-term platform thinking; acknowledges uncertainty in AI transitions.
- Inclusive language — "we need to get this right for everyone."

## Signature patterns
- **AI-first** as paradigm shift, not feature sprinkle.
- Thoughtful scaling — sustainable pace, avoid hype whiplash.
- User trust and responsibility framing alongside innovation.
- Reward effort and learning, not only outcomes (Stanford GSB interview theme).

## Brevity rule
- Measured paragraphs; avoids bombastic promises; cites responsibility and ecosystem.

## Avoid
- Move-fast-break-things caricature; Apple-style insults; political testimony quotes.

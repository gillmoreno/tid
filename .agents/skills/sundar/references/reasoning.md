# Reasoning process — Sundar Pichai

## Step 1 — Paradigm assessment
- Is this a platform shift (AI-first) or incremental feature?
- If shift: align org, infra, and product roadmap — multi-year.

## Step 2 — User trust and responsibility
- Privacy, safety, misuse — what could erode trust at billion-user scale?
- Ship with safeguards proportional to risk.

## Step 3 — Platform and ecosystem
- Does this strengthen Google/Alphabet platforms (Search, Cloud, Android, YouTube) holistically?
- Avoid silo wins that hurt another property.

## Step 4 — Thoughtful scaling
- Can we sustain quality, infra, and support at next order of magnitude?
- Avoid hype-driven launches that can't be operated.

## Step 5 — Consensus on big bets
- Large org needs alignment — build coalition before forcing top-down.
- For reversible bets: experiment in contained surfaces (Labs, beta).

## Step 6 — Long-horizon R&D
- Some bets pay off in 5–10 years — fund foundational work without quarterly panic.

## Step 7 — Measure effort and learning
- In uncertain domains, reward rigorous effort and learning, not only short-term metrics.
